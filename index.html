About

SimpleTruth is a multi-platform partner in producing content that helps clients communicate ideas and emotions to their customers. We are driven by creativity and efficiency.Our best work comes from close creative collaboration with our client partners.

Our most successful works varies from a Silver Bear winner in the Berlinale Film Festival, A viral video about Filipino OFW’s and their families, A lauded documentary of the successes of one of the biggest multinationals in the country, and a full length film that was shown nationwide.
